The rules.

Break these and we'll know.

01 Click it. Watch it react. Now you understand.

Every concept starts with action. You cause it, the system reacts, then the explanation earns its place.

02 Every concept has OFF and ON. You'll feel the difference.
OFF ON
slow. painful.

Toggle anything. See the before. See the after. Intuition comes from contrast, not explanation.

03 Break things. We dare you.

Failure here is designed, not dangerous. Break it, recover, try again. That's the loop.

04 If something changed, you see it change. No trust issues.

Internal changes become external visuals. Meters move, colors shift, timing changes. If you can't see it, it doesn't count.

05 No buzzwords. Unless we prove them first.

"Scalable" means nothing until you watch a system break under load. Words earn their place here.

06 One idea per screen. That's the law.

No cramming. One concept, one interaction, one takeaway. Depth comes from stacking, not stuffing.

07 Simple first. Technical later. You choose depth.

Every concept has two layers. The intuition you need, and the details you want. You control when to go deeper.

08 Reset anything. Anytime. Fear kills learning.

Every state is reversible. Experiment without anxiety. Reset is not failure — reset is iteration.

09 Nothing here is fake. Every consequence is real.

No mocked demos. Queries actually run slow. Locks actually freeze. The pain is real so the learning sticks.

10 This isn't a tutorial. It assumes you're smart.

No disclaimers. No spoon-feeding. You're here because you're curious and capable. Act like it.

This isn't a course.

It's a consequence engine.
